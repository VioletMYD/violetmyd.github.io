I am anti-AI art. My art is not for sale. I do not allow reposts of my art without permission.

As a creative hailing from New York and a recent graduated of the Florida Institute of Recording Sound and Technology, I am continuing to hone my artistic skills forever and always. Although freelance is interesting and fun, I hope to work with a team and have stable morale.

I aim to polish my proficiency in humanoid anatomy, as well as practice and become more comfortable with backgrounds, architecture, animal anatomy, eccentric fashion design, and storytelling.

I am interested in fantastical, mythical, macabre things; some gore, horror, unsettling visuals, plots that range from ridiculous to psychologically deep, and the like. I love to use pops of color and work in dynamic expressions and poses when I have the energy.

My biggest inspirations are Castlevania (by Powerhouse Animation Studios), Jojo's Bizarre Adventure (Araki Hirohiko), Jujutsu Kaisen (Akutami Gege), Case Study of Vanitas (Mochizuki Jun), and Dungeons & Dragons - for which I've fulfilled commissions related to before. I am most comfortable with traditional art as my medium and have been practicing Procreate for years. Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop are recent programs I've had the privilege of exploring, and I will blend what I've learned into my creative process (using free alternatives).

Thank you for reading!
