Not-So-Brief Bio Get In Touch

I am anti-AI art. My art is not for sale. I do not allow reposts of my art without permission.

As a creative hailing from New York and currently a student at the Florida Institute of Recording Sound and Technology, I am continuing to hone my artistic skills forever and always. Although freelance is interesting and fun, I hope to work with a team and have stable morale.

I aim to polish my proficiency in humanoid anatomy, as well as practice and become more comfortable with backgrounds, architecture, animal anatomy, eccentric fashion design, and storytelling.

I am interested in fantastical, mythical, dark things; some gore, horror, unsettling visuals, plots that range from ridiculous to psychologically deep, and the like. I love to use pops of color and work in dynamic expressions and poses when I have the energy.

My biggest inspirations are Castlevania (by Powerhouse Animation Studios), Jojo's Bizarre Adventure (David Production), Case Study of Vanitas (Bones), and Dungeons & Dragons - for which I've fulfilled commissions related to before. I am most comfortable with traditional art as my medium and have been practicing Procreate for years. Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop are recent passions that I've dabbled in and want to mix into my creative process.

Thank you for reading!
